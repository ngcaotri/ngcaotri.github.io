Blog and Learn

Let’s first start with some plant shopping. It’s very easy to find some nice plants in this city! You can go to the Urban Plants, or visit one of the cool urban plant shops downtown. Our list was long and between meetings & lunches we managed to visit quite a few. Our first stop is at the beautiful Urban Plants, by talented Tris, the Vietnamese plant artist and creative director behind Gardent Market also known as JJ. If you like his signature style of unique vintage treasures and plants, make sure to also check out our post about the Green Fingers plant shop in Dalat HERE.

Urban Plants in every size, shape and color, a repotting station, cooled flowers, airplants, a really nice selection of ceramics, glassware, plant pots, plant hangers, tools, soils, stones & gems, terrariums and more at this XXL shop with exposed bricks and bright yellow, pink and black accents. The design of the shop is spacious, clean and makes the plants stand out really well.
